# Agenthoryx Research Consortium

## Overview
The Agenthoryx Research Consortium bridges the gap between academic theory and enterprise deployment. We fund, coordinate, and publish research surrounding AI-native computing infrastructures.

## Core Participants
- **Universities**: Leading computer science departments focused on programming languages and AI.
- **Research Labs**: Organizations researching AGI, multi-agent frameworks, and AI safety.
- **Industry Partners**: Tech enterprises building scalable AI solutions using Agenthoryx.

## Focus Areas
1. **AI Runtime Optimization**: Reducing the latency between deterministic execution and LLM inference.
2. **Multi-Agent Systems**: Developing mathematical models for conflict resolution among autonomous agents.
3. **Language Design**: Exploring new syntax paradigms for embedding neural operations natively.
4. **Distributed Intelligence**: Optimizing task scheduling across a 10,000+ node agent cluster.
5. **Human-AI Collaboration**: Designing secure interfaces for human oversight of autonomous workflows.
